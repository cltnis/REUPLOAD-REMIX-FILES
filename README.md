1. (BIG REMINDER!!!) Make sure you and your friend followed the 32.11 tutorial FIRST that i made last Thursday (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BEH5gZUyr0I)
2. the Hoster and your friend must download Radmin VPN from [here](https://www.radmin-vpn.com/)
3. Once you and your friend downloaded and installed Radmin VPN, you and your friend should see this screen (the My PC and fake IP Address on the bottom will look different for you and your friend) <img width="308" height="434" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/afec3588-0c0e-402c-829f-b71deb10c971" />

4. Now the hoster will click on Create Network
5. Once the hoster clicked on Create Network, the hoster should see this <img width="253" height="117" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bff4ac1f-c7ec-4503-b6bc-783a2d9d7fd8" />

6. Now the hoster put in your Network name (basically any name u want) and the password (you will have to retype it btw) (these 2 are for when your friend is joining you)
7. Now the hoster click on Create
8. now hoster, send the info (Network name and password) to your friend
9. when your friend goes on Radmin VPN, your friend should click on Join Network (on the bottom of this image) <img width="308" height="434" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/afec3588-0c0e-402c-829f-b71deb10c971" />
10. then MAKE SURE your friend has it on Private Network and NOT on Gaming Network, then your friend type the Network name and password the hoster sent you
11. Then click Join
12. now hoster next download the Neonite Backend (or you can download the Custom Neonite Backend which has some gamemode changes and the reload lootpool hotfixes and unvaulted items)
13. hoster if you already have it installed, skip 12 and double click on "Start Neonite.bat" (MAKE SURE TO LEAVE IT RUNNING!!!)
14. your friend must go on the Custom Reboot Launcher and click on Backend
15. your friend must change the type of Backend to Remote
16. then your friend must copy the hoster's fake IP Address (by right clicking on the hoster's name and clicking on "Copy IP Address)
17. then go back on the Custom Reboot Launcher and where it says host, change the host name to the hoster's fake IP Address (by pasting the fake IP Address onto the Host textbox)
18. For Port, make sure its on 5595 and NOT 3551
19. then your friend should click on Start Backend and they should be connected
20. now hoster, go on releases on the github repo and choose any gamemode you want to do (btw Event gamemode does work on Multiplayer)
21. hoster, give ONLY the console.dll and Starfall.dll to your friend
22. now friend, go to settings then internal files and then hit the folder icon on the "Unreal Engine patcher" and put "console.dll" in there by selecting the file, then hit the folder icon on "Authentication patcher" and put "Starfall.dll" in there by selecting the file.
23. now hoster, make sure you connected to the Neonite Backend by going to backend and make sure the type is on Local and the Port is 5595 and NOT 3551
24. now hoster and friend, go back on Custom Reboot Launcher and go to the Play section and click on Launch Fortnite
25. now hoster, click on ignore when prompted and friend, also click on ignore when prompted
26. once the hoster and friend is ingame, hoster go to the Host tab on click on "Start hosting"
27. once the gameserver is loaded successfully, hoster hit the ` on your keyboard (or F8) (or for smaller keyboard: FN + 8) and type "open 127.0.0.1" (without the "")
28. now hoster once your ingame, friend go on Radmin VPN and MAKE SURE you copied your hoster's fake IP Address
29. friend, hit the ` on your keyboard (or F8) (or for smaller keyboard: FN + 8) and type "open hostersip" (without the "") (basically hostersip is the IP you copied from the hoster)
30. now hoster and your friend should be ingame!
